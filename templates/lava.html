Lava Kunfa is a coffee shop in El-mashtal street in Khartoum and in Omdurman in Al-wadi street. It sells different types of delicious Kunafas with different combinations and that includes: cheese, chocolates, fruits and many other varieties. Lava also provides many type of beverages and juices.

Nymeria Technology worked with Lava to develop and integrate a POS system in order to support and maintain the daily operation of Lava in terms of sales, purchasing, and the management of inventory.

Our POS system helps the restaurant and coffee shop to have a robust system to track sales that are based on items or categories to make track shifts based on cashiers over a specific time period, tracking the inventory by adding item recipients and that is been detected from the inventory based on the sales.
