Gester is restaurant located near Ibrahim Maliks hospatial. It serves the medical staff and patients. Due to the high demand the owner was looking fora better way to track and follow up with the updates about his restaurant, he worked with Nymeria Technology and he is happy more than ever .

Nymeria Technology worked with Gester to develop and integrate a POS system in order to support and maintain the daily operation of Gester in terms of sales, purchasing, and the management of inventory.

Our POS system helps the restaurant and coffee shop to have a robust system to track sales that are based on items or categories to make track shifts based on cashiers over a specific time period, tracking the inventory by adding item recipients and that is been detected from the inventory based on the sales.
