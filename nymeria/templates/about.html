Our Story

Mambo;

Nymeria was founded on the 5th of July, 2018; that makes it a Cancer in the celestial universe. Much like the cofounders, Nymeria is inspired by the moon, a traveler; or so the legend goes. The company aspires to amplify the voice of women and empower them as Nymeria's founding father was raised by four powerful women.

The cofounders of Nymeria met in 2009 and always dreamt of creating something tangible that would allow for growth in the future. Not all stories have happy endings, as the co-founders split. However, Nymeria was the seed that was left behind.

Alas, the seed was planted and grew through the hard work required of a single, consistent nurturer.

Here is Nymeria: a dire wolf, a luna, a worrier, a queen. Greeting all with "Mambo"; as the team is based in Africa and it carries the authenticity and ethnicity of Africa throughout its work.

With all the Love and Respect,
